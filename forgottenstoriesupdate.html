Update Overview

Hello everyone. Welcome to the probably (?) last news post before we have the official release of Pirate-Arena. To start things off, we have 1 new character and 2 reworks, along with a slight balance change to cover some of the existing holes in the game. We will also be showcasing: Patreon Rewards, Jabra Compensation Rewards, along with the updates to the Battle Pass reward (new rank hat changes). I recommend using the Table Of Contents to see what you like to see.

Ending Notes

This should be the final news post before the official release of Pirate-Arena. I'm grateful to the Helpers and all testers. Whether we succeed or fail, we have been able to successfully create something new yet familiar in arena games. Whether the journey or short or long, what's important is that the journey was created. To help cement this change, I'll be explaining a small story about Pirate-Arena.

When Pirate-Arena was first created, it was called "One Piece Arena". Halfway down the line, I learned that another One Piece Arena had already existed, so I changed the name to Pirate-Arena. I have no intention of discounting their work however I had already began working on Pirate-Arena with the ex-owner Beneh.
